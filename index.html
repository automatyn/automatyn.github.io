FAQ

Common questions

Do I need technical skills?

No. You fill in a form about your business and the AI builds your bot. There is nothing to code, no flow builders, and no technical setup. If you can fill in a web form, you can do this.

Can I use my existing WhatsApp number?

Yes. You connect your WhatsApp Business account by scanning a QR code. Your existing number stays the same and customers message you as usual.

What happens when the bot cannot answer?

The bot tells the customer it will pass their message to you, and you get a notification. You can jump into the conversation at any time. The bot never makes things up.

What types of businesses use this?

Salons, clinics, gyms, restaurants, cleaning services, tutoring centres, trades, and any small business that gets customer questions on WhatsApp. If you answer the same questions repeatedly, this is for you.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. There are no contracts and no cancellation fees. You can cancel from your dashboard in one click. The free plan has no credit card requirement at all.
