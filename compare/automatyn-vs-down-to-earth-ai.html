Automatyn vs Down To Earth AI: WhatsApp Agent Comparison for UK Trades

Two UK-built AI agents that answer WhatsApp messages for plumbers, electricians, gardeners and other trades. The pricing models are very different. Here is the side-by-side, with the full setup cost included.

The short answer

Down To Earth AI is a managed service for UK tradesmen. The pricing is £299 one-time setup, then £45 a month for the AI Receptionist, plus a separate £23 a month for the WhatsApp add-on. Total monthly cost with WhatsApp enabled is £68 a month, plus the £299 upfront.

Automatyn is self-serve. Starter is free with 25 conversations a month. Pro is £29 a month with 150 conversations. Max is £69 a month with unlimited conversations. WhatsApp is included on every plan. No setup fee. No add-ons.

If you want a managed service where someone configures the agent for you and you pay a one-time setup fee, Down To Earth AI is the right shape. If you want to try the agent yourself for free, then pay £29 a month if it works, Automatyn is the right shape.

Managed vs self-serve

Down To Earth AI describes itself as "100% done for you." Their setup fee covers configuring the agent against your specific business, your service area, your pricing, your protocols. You hand off the work, they build, you start receiving polished WhatsApp replies.

Automatyn is self-serve. You answer a configuration sheet, the agent goes live, and you tune the responses by sending feedback to the agent during the first few days. There is no person assigned to set you up. The trade-off is no setup fee and a permanent free tier where you can test on real messages.

Neither approach is wrong. They suit different owners. A trade business owner who hates configuring software and is happy to pay £299 to never touch it will prefer Down To Earth AI. An owner who wants to start free, see what the agent says to a real client, and only pay if it works will prefer Automatyn.
