Both promise to answer client messages on autopilot. The pricing structure, channel focus, and target customer are very different. Here is what actually matters for a UK plumber, electrician, gas engineer, or tree surgeon comparing the two.

The short answer

Trillet is a US-based, voice-first AI receptionist starting at $49 a month with 150 minutes of voice included. WhatsApp is bundled but secondary. Automatyn is a UK-focused, WhatsApp-first AI agent starting free with 25 conversations a month, then $29 a month for 150 conversations on Pro and $79 a month unlimited on Max. Channel focus

Trillet was built for inbound phone calls first. WhatsApp is included on the base plan, which is unusual for a US voice tool, but the product is optimised around call handling. Their feature list emphasises "answers within 2 seconds", simultaneous call handling, and call forwarding from existing phone numbers.

Automatyn was built for WhatsApp first. Voice is on the roadmap but not the focus. Most UK trade businesses already publish a WhatsApp number on their van, Google profile, and Checkatrade listing. The clients message that number. Automatyn answers it.

Setup time

Trillet claims a 5-minute setup with automatic website scanning, which works well when your trade business has a populated site with services, hours, and pricing. If your website is a single page or non-existent, the scan has less to work with.

Automatyn quotes a 10-minute setup directly on your WhatsApp Business number. No website required. You answer a short configuration sheet, the agent goes live, and you can intercept any reply before it sends for the first day if you want a safety net.

Who each one suits

Trillet suits you if: you take most enquiries by phone, you have a populated business website, you operate in the United States, you want appointment booking via voice, and the $49 entry tier is comfortable.

Automatyn suits you if: you take most enquiries on WhatsApp, you operate in the UK, you want to try before paying, your clients send photos and voice notes that need to be acknowledged, or you want unlimited conversations at a fixed monthly price under £80.

What Trillet does better

Voice handling and concurrent call capacity. Trillet's pitch around picking up unlimited simultaneous calls is real and matters if your phone has been your main funnel. The 28-day money-back window is also longer than Automatyn's 7-day window.

What Automatyn does better

WhatsApp-native handling, including images and voice notes from clients. UK-specific pricing in GBP. A free Starter plan so you can put the agent in front of real clients without paying. Unlimited tier at $79 a month with no per-conversation overage. Built specifically for UK trade verticals (plumbers, electricians, gardeners, roofers, tree surgeons, gas engineers).

The bottom line

If you are a UK trade business, the decision is straightforward: try Automatyn's free Starter plan, see whether the agent handles your real Saturday-night messages, and only consider Trillet if you decide voice is the channel that matters more than WhatsApp. If you are in the US and phone-first, Trillet is the better fit and the comparison stops there.
